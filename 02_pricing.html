Frequently asked

Common questions.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Monthly rolls cancel at end of cycle. Upfront plans cancel within 14 days for a full refund, otherwise run to the end of the committed term.

Do I need a trade licence to start?+

No licence needed to sign up or build your store. You only need to upload it when you're ready to go live and start accepting orders — usually within 48 hours.

Can I sell in KSA and Oman from a UAE-registered store?+

Yes. One dashboard, three markets. Arabic + English out of the box. Aramex handles the cross-border shipping.

What happens if I run out of AI credits?+

Top up from the Wallet in one click. Credits never expire. Product media costs 10 credits, social content 15, 3D models 20.

How does VAT work?+

The platform calculates UAE 5% VAT automatically once you enable it in settings. It tracks your rolling 12-month sales against the AED 375,000 threshold so you know exactly when to register.

Can I migrate from Shopify?+

Yes. Import products via Excel, CSV, or the AI image extractor that reads product photos and builds the listing automatically. Most brands are fully migrated within a week.

Who owns my customer data?+

You do. Export everything anytime. API access on the yearly plan gives you programmatic access to orders, products, and customers.

Is there a setup fee?+

No. The price you see is everything. No hidden onboarding cost, no platform migration charge, no integration fee.
