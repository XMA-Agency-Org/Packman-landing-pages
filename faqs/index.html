Getting started

How long does it take to launch a store?

From a single product photo, most sellers are live in under 10 minutes. Packman auto-generates your storefront, content, and first collection. Most see their first paid order within 48 hours.

Do I need my own domain?

No. Every store starts on a free shop.packman.ai/yourstore domain. You can connect a custom domain at any time from your dashboard.

Is my store bilingual by default?

Yes. Packman stores are Arabic and English out of the box, with proper RTL layout and translation of your content.

Do I need a trade licence?

Not to start. You can sign up, build your store, and even take your first orders without one. When you're ready to scale, you upload your trade licence inside the dashboard — we walk you through it.

Pricing & billing

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every store starts with a 14-day free trial. After that, you pick monthly or yearly billing. Shipping labels and optional add-ons are billed separately.

What's the difference between monthly and yearly?

The yearly plan saves you 25%. And if you complete 365 orders in your first year on the yearly plan, the next year is free.

Do you charge transaction fees?

No. Packman itself charges zero commission on your sales. Payment gateways (Tabby, Tamara, Apple Pay, etc.) have their own per-transaction fees set by them.

Can I switch plans?

You can upgrade your plan at any time. Switch from monthly to yearly to lock in the 25% savings.

Fulfillment & shipping

Where are your warehouses?

We operate fulfillment centers in UAE (Dubai · Al Quoz), KSA (Riyadh and Jeddah), and Oman (Muscat). You can stock in one or all of them.

Which countries do you ship to?

We ship to 160+ countries. International rates are quoted live at checkout based on weight, destination, and service level.

How fast is regional delivery?

Same-day in Dubai and Riyadh, next-day across the GCC, and 3–5 day standard delivery internationally.

Payments

What payment methods are supported?

Cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, COD, Tabby, Tamara, and Mada. More are added regularly.

How fast are payouts?

Payouts to your bank account every 2 weeks on standard plans. Faster cycles available on enterprise.

Support & account

How do I contact support?

Live chat in your Packman dashboard, email at support@packman.ai, or WhatsApp. All bilingual, 24/7.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Products, orders, customers, and settings can be exported as CSV at any time.
