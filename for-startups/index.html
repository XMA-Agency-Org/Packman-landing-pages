First-timer questions

You're not the only one asking.

Do I need a trade licence to sign up?+

No. Sign up with just an email. You only need a trade licence when you're ready to go live — usually 24–48 hours after you start building.

What if I only have one product?+

Perfect. Many of our best-performing stores launched with one SKU. Add variants, add photos, go live. You can grow the catalog from there.

Can I still sell on Instagram and WhatsApp?+

Yes. Packman is your back-end — it doesn't replace your social presence. Drive Instagram traffic to your Packman store, use Draft Carts to send pre-built checkout links via WhatsApp to specific customers.

What payment methods can I accept?+

Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Card on Delivery, Cash on Delivery, Tabby (BNPL), Tamara (BNPL). Turn any of them on or off in settings.

Do I need my own warehouse?+

No. Ship your stock to a Packman warehouse (UAE, KSA, or Oman — AED 2,000/yr per pallet). We pick, pack, and hand to the courier. You see every movement in the dashboard.

What if I don't know how to build a store?+

The Builder is drag-and-drop. Pick a theme. Add products. Publish. Our Learning Center has 14 guides. And the 3-month Account Management add-on (AED 2,400) gets you a human who builds it with you.
